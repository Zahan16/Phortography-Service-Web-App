Malcolm Lismore

Welcome to the world of Malcolm Lismore Photography! Based on the breathtaking North West coast of Scotland, Malcolm is a passionate freelance photographer dedicated to capturing the beauty and majesty of the natural world. With a keen eye for detail and a deep love for the outdoors, Malcolm's work primarily focuses on the stunning Scottish landscapes, its diverse wildlife, and the enchanting coastal birds that inhabit this rugged region.

Passion for Nature

Malcolm's greatest passion lies in showcasing the untamed beauty of Scotland. From the dramatic cliffs and rolling hills to the serene lochs and dense forests, he captures the essence of this unique landscape in every shot. His wildlife photography brings the viewer up close and personal with the animals and birds that call Scotland home, offering a glimpse into their natural habitats and behaviors.

Versatility in Photography

While Malcolm's heart may be in nature photography, his skills extend far beyond. Like many talented photographers, he offers his services for a variety of other photographic needs. Whether it's the joy and intimacy of a wedding, the personality and charm of a portrait, or the energy and excitement of a special event, Malcolm brings the same level of dedication and artistic vision to every project he undertakes.

Professional Services

  • Weddings: Capturing the magic and emotion of your special day with timeless, elegant photos that tell your love story.
  • Portraits: Creating stunning, personalized portraits that reflect the true essence of his subjects.
  • Events: Documenting events with a keen eye for detail, ensuring every important moment is beautifully preserved.

Contact

For inquiries about Malcolm's services, or any other questions, please contact him. Malcolm looks forward to sharing his vision with you and capturing your most cherished moments.
